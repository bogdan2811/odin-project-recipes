Easy Chicken Piccata Orzo Casserole

Description

What's a chicken piccata? It’s pan-fried chicken in a piccata sauce, which is a combination of butter, oil, fresh lemon juice, and capers. Simple, right? The secret to making the best chicken piccata is having the perfect amount of lemon, and I’ve got you covered with this really easy chicken recipe.

You can make this dreamy dish two different ways, and we’ve got instructions for both! One is a creamy chicken piccata, and the other is not. They’re both extremely tasty. The cream takes it to the next level, but depending on your mood or dietary restrictions, you may want to try it without!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Garlic powder
  • Flour
  • Olive oil and butter
  • Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Cut chicken breasts in half lengthwise to make thinner cutlets.
  2. Sprinkle them with salt & pepper and garlic powder, then dredge them in the flour. Brown on both sides.
  3. Remove them from the pan and add the chicken broth, lemon juice, butter, and capers, scraping up the brown bits.
  4. Stir in the cream. Return the chicken to the pan, and simmer until the sauce has reduced and the chicken is cooked through. Easy peasy!
